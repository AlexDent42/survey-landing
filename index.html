How to Start a Web App in 2022? — World’s First Online Research on Web App Development EVER!

Global
research

This is the world's first research on how to build a web app in 2022! The survey is absolutely anonymous and has 20 questions related to web development. If you are planning to build a web app, whether you are an experienced software engineer or have a proven background, you are still welcome to take part! Our aim is to make the survey as representative as possible thanks to the diversity of programmers worldwide.

Survey
Audience

We shared this online survey in various tech communities, groups, and socials, and we’re happy to see among its audience people from newbies in the sphere of tech, students and coding amateurs, to large scale companies and enterprises, fulfilling their tech needs required to build their web apps faster and better.

Data
Analysis

Our analysis can help you understand the market of web development, get inspired and gain some insights to start your web app building by guiding you through the whole application building process and show you the basics of web development.

The Purpose of
the Survey

We implement robust processes in software development and this survey is a way to learn more about popular modern patterns of web applications creation.

Survey Flow

By collecting data from hundreds of developers, we will understand the recent trends and modern patterns of web app creation used by coders and we will find out more about the tools and approaches used to start web apps in 2022.

Research Structure

This online research is based on an online survey that includes 20 clear questions about web development carefully handpicked by our software engineers.

Survey Coverage

We want to help everyone, from students and young developers to the real experts in programming, from budding startups to large companies and enterprises, to learn more about how to create their web apps faster.

Feedback

We highly value your feedback. Please drop us a line if you would like to provide some comments on this or that question.

Survey Results 🚩

We will share the results in our socials by the end of May, 2022.
We deliberately did not specify the type of web app in question, making it open-ended. You can answer the questions just as if they were asked about your next web app project. Think of a CMS or CRM, e-commerce store, SAAS app, or admin panel, or whatever you are going to build next.
